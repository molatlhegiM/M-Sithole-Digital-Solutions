Learn About Our Journey

2016 The Beginning of M-Sithole Holdings

M-Sithole Holdings was officially registered, marking the beginning of our business journey with a primary focus on logistics under the name M-Sithole Logistics.

2017 Launch of Newday Internet Café

We diversified our business focus, launching Newday Internet Café to provide essential internet and document services for our community.

2020 Pursuit of a Coffee Business

During the pandemic, we paused Tipsy Bartenders SA and ventured into a mobile coffee business franchise, although it later closed due to unforeseen challenges.

2021 Freelance Coffee Services

Returning to the coffee industry, we offered freelance services within the coffee business community in Sandton, focusing on adaptability and connection.

2024 Launch of M-Sithole Digital Solutions

In 2024, M-Sithole Digital Solutions was born, channeling our experience into a dedicated digital marketing agency, ready to elevate brands in the digital space.

M-Sithole Digital Solutions – Professional digital marketing services including web design, SEO, social media management, and content creation for businesses worldwide.
